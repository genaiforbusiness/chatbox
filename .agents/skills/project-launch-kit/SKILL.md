# PoC Launch Kit & Team Reflection Guide

This skill helps you prepare your final product presentation materials and reflect on your team's semester journey.

## 1. Deliverables Checklist

### 1. The Video Pitch (3-5 mins)
- Focus: Persuasive, marketing-style pitch to potential users and sponsors.
- Content: Explain the business problem, show a demo of the core app features, and articulate the value proposition.
- AI tools are highly encouraged for scripting, voiceovers, background music, or animations.
- Submission: Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or Vimeo, and place the link prominently at the top of your `README.md`.

### 2. The Quick-Start Guide: `QUICK_START.md`
- Create a file named `QUICK_START.md` in the root of the repository.
- Focus: Practical, user-facing instructions written for a non-technical audience.
- Content: High-level overview of the application and simple step-by-step instructions for using 2-3 of its key features.

### 3. The Reflective Team Interview (15 mins)
Prepare to discuss these questions as a team during the final class round-table:
- **Process & Collaboration**: How did `GOVERNANCE.md` hold up? Where did AI save time or change direction? What was hardest about managing an AI project?
- **Technical & Conceptual**: Can you explain RAG in 30 seconds to a non-technical manager? What are the key limitations of current LLMs? What misconceptions did you start with?
- **Critical Thinking & Outlook**: Where was the line between using AI as a collaborator vs. as a crutch? What are your business hopes and worries for AI?
