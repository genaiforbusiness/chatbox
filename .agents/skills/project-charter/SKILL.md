# Project Charter Guide

This skill helps your team draft the three foundational documents that define the Business Case, Product Features, and Operating Model of your project.

## 1. Deliverables Checklist

### 1. The Business Case: `README.md`
Your project's front page must include:
- **Mission Statement**: A single, clear sentence summarizing the project's purpose.
- **The Problem**: A description of the high-value business problem or user pain point.
- **Our Solution**: A high-level overview of how your AI application solves the problem.
- **Target User**: A description of the primary user persona.

### 2. The Product Features: `USER_STORIES.md`
Translate your idea into prioritized features:
- **User Persona**: Detailed description of the user's role, goals, and frustrations.
- **Epics & User Stories**: List of major features (Epics) broken down into user stories in the format:
  > *"As a **[type of user]**, I want to **[perform some action]** so that I can **[achieve some goal]**."*

### 3. The Operating Model: `GOVERNANCE.md`
Establish team process and accountability:
- **Team Roles & Responsibilities**: Define roles for the current stage.
- **Collaboration Protocol**: Rules for GitHub usage (Issues, Projects, Discussions) and meeting schedules.
- **AI Usage Strategy**:
  - *Task Delegation*: When to use ad-hoc assistance vs. systematic automation.
  - *Collaboration & Citation*: Policy for verifying and citing AI contributions.
- **Decision-Making Framework**: How you will resolve disagreements about technical or product directions.
